About Us

Hi there! We're Moonpie & Minmin, a couple who's been through our fair share of challenges but always come out stronger on the other side. Our journey is an emotional rollercoaster, but we always find a way to support each other and stay committed.

Moonpie is the dreamer, finding beauty in the little things. With a passion for storytelling and a heart full of wonder, Moonpie brings our experiences to life in the most creative and charming ways.

Minmin is the planner, turning dreams into reality with careful planning and a keen eye for detail. Minmin's dedication ensures we navigate our challenges with grace and determination.

Together, we hope to inspire you to stay strong and embrace your own journey. Through our shared experiences and moments of joy, we want to make the world a little brighter for everyone.

Thanks for being part of our adventure. Let's face life's challenges together and create something beautiful!
